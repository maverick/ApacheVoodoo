Net::DNS (which is used by Email::Valid) carelessly overwrites $_ in several places.
To neutralize this effect, $_ is localized before the call to Email::Valid.
Patching information to fix Net::DNS is listed below:

In Net::DNS::Question replace
	my $qname = defined ($_ = shift) ? $_ : '';
with
	my $qname = shift;
	$qname = '' unless defined ($qname);

In Net::DNS::Resolver::Base in the subroutines _ip_is_ipv4 and _ip_is_ipv6 replace
	$_ = shift;
with
	local $_ = shift;
